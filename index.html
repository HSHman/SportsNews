About Us

Created in 2020 by a handful of committed individuals as a solution to providing quick and easy sports information, Sports News was founded by people just like you — amateur sports fanatics. Since then, the Toronto-based company has grown – and continues to grow — steadily to a staff of well over 3 developers. Our website is updated around the clock as information breaks, often in the form of games or press releases from major professional sports teams in North America.

Future Developments

To Include video highlights and to expand to other sports leagues such as NHL, NFL, MLS, and MLB. As well as to be featured on top news websites such as the New York Times and television shows such as ESPN’s SportsCenter.
